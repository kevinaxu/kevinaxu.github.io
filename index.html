Iguazu Falls

This past Thursday we packed up our things, nestled into our double-decker bus, and sat for the next 18 hours on the way to Iguazu National Park in Misiones Argentina. Misiones, one of 23 provinces in Argentina, is wedged in the most north eastern part of the country between Paraguay and Brasil. To get to the actual national park, you have to travel all the way through the Misiones countryside until you reach the point where all the countries meet.
